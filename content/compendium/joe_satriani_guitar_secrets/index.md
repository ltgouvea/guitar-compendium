*Guitar Secrets* is a comprehensive collection of 41 private lessons by Joe Satriani, originally featured as a monthly column in *Guitar For The Practicing Musician* magazine. Spanning three years of installments, this book covers a vast array of topics including advanced improvisation, unusual tunings, harmonic techniques, fretboard visualization, and music theory. Designed to be concise and immediately applicable, each lesson provides unique insights into Satriani's technical and creative approach to the guitar, featuring everything from fundamental chord studies to experimental concepts like "Atonal Scat Singing" and "Photographic Memory" for modes.
