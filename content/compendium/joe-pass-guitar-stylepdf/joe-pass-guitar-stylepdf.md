Joe Pass's "Guitar Style" is a comprehensive guide to jazz guitar, emphasizing improvisation and a practical understanding of the instrument. The book is divided into harmony and melody sections. Pass condenses complex theory into direct applications for the working musician, covering chord construction, embellishment, and substitution. He stresses the importance of ear training and the relationship between chords and melodic lines, providing a foundation for intelligent improvisation.
