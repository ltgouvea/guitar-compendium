In this second volume, Guthrie Govan continues his exploration of high-level guitar playing, diving deeper into specialized techniques. The book explores advanced concepts such as alternate picking, sweep and economy picking, tapping, and emulating other instruments, providing experienced players with the tools to further refine their technical proficiency and expressive capabilities.
