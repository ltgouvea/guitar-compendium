This foundational volume is designed to teach students how to read music and develop dexterity in both hands through original compositions and exercises. It covers essential topics such as tuning, basic music theory, position playing, and fundamental chord forms. The method emphasizes gradual progress and regular review to achieve speed and accuracy on the instrument.
