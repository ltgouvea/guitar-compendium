Mick Goodrick's "The Advancing Guitarist" is a seminal work that shifts away from traditional method books, offering instead a vast collection of concepts for guitarists to explore. It covers essential topics like fingerboard mechanics, including single-string and position playing, alongside advanced harmonic materials like triads and quartal harmony. The book encourages self-discovery and a deep understanding of the instrument's complexity through practical exercises and insightful commentaries.
