This is the second module of the guitar course from IG&T (Instituto de Guitarra e Tecnologia). The material focuses on intermediate guitar techniques, including finger independence exercises (1-2-3-4 chromatic patterns) and fundamental studies designed to develop technical proficiency and coordination on the instrument.
