## The anti-AI on music manifesto

- Music is a sacred art form and one of the most beautiful (if not THE most) displays of our human capabilities.
- However, contrary to a more forgiving line of thought, MAKING and PLAYING good music is NOT for everyone.
- This is not because of a mere skill difference or god-given talent.
- It is about giving yourself in on a lifelong commitment to improve everyday accepting to possibly never sounding exactly how you think you could.
- Without the chaos and the impredictability of the human mind creativity, there is no music.
- Without the HUMAN SOUL, there is no music.
- No technology comes before the real artist.
- AI-Made "music" is nothing but a cheap imitation of stolen art from real people.
- Digital platforms surfing the AI wave giving more money to fake artists than struggling real musicians should be extinct.
- Making money through AI generated shitty music is a dishonor.
- ROBOTS ARE UNCAPABLE OF MAKING MUSIC.
- IF YOU DEPEND ON ROBOTS TO "CREATE" MUSIC, YOU ARE TOO INCOMPETENT TO BE A MUSICIAN. FUCK OFF. WE EXPECT WAY MORE THAN THAT EVEN FOR STARTERS.
